The Diet

Phase 1

For 30 days, you will avoid the foods listed on your food sensitivity test AND the following foods:

  • Dairy (no milk, cheese, yogurt, ghee, butter, margarine, shortening, goat’s milk)
  • Eggs
  • Gluten (no wheat, oats, rye, barley which are typically found in breads, pasta, cereals)
  • Tomatoes, tomato sauce, or anything with tomatoes
  • Dehydrated fruit (usually has preservatives and sugar)
  • Rice
  • Corn
  • Potatoes
  • Alcohol
  • Coffee, black tea, soda (all caffeine)
  • Fruit juices
  • Iodized salt (table)
  • Sugar, natural & artificial sweeteners (including brown sugar, honey, stevia, Splenda, agave, monk fruit, erythritol)
  • Soy or products containing soy (including Tofu)
  • Beef, pork, shellfish, cold cuts, bacon, hot dogs, canned meat, sausage, game meat
  • Lentils
  • Chocolate
  • Dates
  • Peanuts

Phase 2

In your next appointment, you will make a plan with Dr. Shannon to reintroduce foods not on your food sensitivity list.


Why am I on this diet?

  1. To decrease inflammation and improve your health!
  2. To find out what foods are affecting your body's health
  3. To learn how to find and choose healthy food in the store, including how to read ingredient lists
  4. To make sure you are drinking enough water every day (drink 1/2 of your body’s weight)
  5. To learn to include protein and vegetables in your meals
  6. Learn more about the benefits of the elimination diet in Dr. Shannon’s blog: Food Sensitivities
