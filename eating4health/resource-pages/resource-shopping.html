Shopping: Where do I buy...?


Now that you are eating differently and probably purchasing some new ingredients, here are some ideas of where to pick up different items.


Items at Costco:
  • Organic almond butter
  • Nuts (almonds, walnuts, pecans)
  • Organic canned chicken
  • Fresh and frozen vegetables & fruits
  • Coconut oil
  • Olive oil
  • Avocado oil
  • Zucchini noodles (frozen section)
  • Riced cauliflower (frozen section)
  • Canned tuna
  • Hemp seeds
  • Chia seeds

Items at Trader Joe’s:
  • Organic hummus (garlic or spicy hummus)
  • Nuts (almonds, walnuts, pecans, macademia)
  • Cold cuts: sliced turkey withour preservatives
  • Fresh and frozen vegetables & fruits
  • Cassava flour chips
  • Jicama wraps (fridge section)
  • Broccoli slaw (fridge section)
  • Riced cauliflower (frozen section)
  • Sunflower butter
  • Almond butter
  • Coleslaw (fridge section)
