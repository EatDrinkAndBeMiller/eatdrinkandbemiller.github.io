About Apex

Apex Automotive is an independent and locally-owned automotive service and repair shop built on a word-of-mouth reputation. Our ASE-Certified technicians average over 19 years experience. We believe that routine maintenance is the best way to protect your car against issues.

We utilize Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) parts and dealership level diagnostic equipment unless instructed otherwise by our customer. Apex Automotive can address all of your mechanical needs from preventative maintenance to major engine repairs.

Our technicians will inspect your car, diagnose the problem and suggest a repair or replacement. We also include a limited warranty with all the work we do and parts we use.

Warranty

We stand behind the work we do with a 12 month or 12,000 mile warranty for parts and labor. Some parts carry a longer warranty offered by the manufacturer.
